DIGITAL TWIN

Posted on 12th October 2021

A digital twin is a digital representation of a physical object, person, or process, contextualized in a digital version of its environment. Digital twins can help an organization simulate real situations and their outcomes, ultimately allowing it to make better decisions. Digital twins can allow for rapid iterations and optimizations of product designs—far faster than physically testing every single prototype. We’re using Siemens software such as Tecnomatix , NX, Solid Edge, Simcenter 3D, Amesim, Valor and others to deliver Products and Engineering services.
